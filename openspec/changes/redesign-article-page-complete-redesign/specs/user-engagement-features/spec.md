# User Engagement Features Specification

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Advanced Social Sharing System

The article page SHALL provide comprehensive sharing capabilities optimized for Indonesian social media platforms and communication preferences.

#### Scenario: Platform-Specific Sharing

Given users wanting to share articles on different platforms
When they initiate sharing
Then the system should provide customized sharing options for WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn with platform-appropriate formatting and preview generation

#### Scenario: Content-Specific Sharing

Given users reading long articles
When they want to share specific sections
Then they should be able to share direct links to specific article sections with highlighted content and contextual information

#### Scenario: Professional Citation Sharing

Given dental professionals and students
When they share educational content
Then citation-ready sharing options should include proper formatting, author information, publication dates, and reference-style links

### Requirement: Personal Bookmarking System

The article system SHALL allow users to bookmark and organize articles for future reference with intelligent categorization.

#### Scenario: Article Bookmarking

Given users reading valuable content
When they bookmark articles
Then the system should save their reading position, allow personal notes, and categorize bookmarks automatically by topic and tags

#### Scenario: Bookmark Collections and Organization

Given users with multiple saved articles
When they manage their bookmarks
Then they should be able to create custom collections, add personal tags, and search through their saved content

#### Scenario: Reading List Syncing

Given users who access content from multiple devices
When they save bookmarks
Then their reading lists should sync across devices with offline access to bookmarked articles

### Requirement: Interactive Feedback System

The article page SHALL provide multiple channels for user feedback and engagement with content.

#### Scenario: Content Rating System

Given users who have read articles
When they finish reading
Then they should be able to rate content usefulness, accuracy, and relevance with both quantitative ratings and qualitative feedback

#### Scenario: Professional Q&A Integration

Given dental professionals and students
When they read educational content
Then they should be able to ask questions that can be answered by authors or other dental professionals with threaded discussions

#### Scenario: Community Discussion Features

Given health education articles
When users engage with content
Then moderated comment sections should facilitate discussion while maintaining professional and medical accuracy standards

### Requirement: Reading Achievement and Progress System

The article system SHALL include gamification elements to encourage continuous learning and engagement.

#### Scenario: Learning Paths and Achievements

Given users following dental health education
When they complete articles on specific topics
Then they should earn achievements, certificates of completion, and progress tracking toward learning milestones

#### Scenario: Personalized Learning Recommendations

Given users with specific interests or knowledge gaps
When they interact with the article system
Then personalized recommendations should guide them through relevant content based on their reading history and stated interests

#### Scenario: Professional Development Tracking

Given dental students and professionals
When they read educational content
Then the system should track continuing education hours, professional development topics, and generate completion certificates

### Requirement: Content Personalization Engine

The article system SHALL adapt content presentation and recommendations based on user preferences and behavior.

#### Scenario: Adaptive Content Difficulty

Given users with different levels of dental knowledge
When they read articles
Then the system should offer simplified explanations, advanced details, or professional-level content based on their knowledge level

#### Scenario: Personalized Content Feed

Given returning users
When they visit the articles section
Then the homepage and article listings should be personalized based on their reading history, bookmarks, and engagement patterns

#### Scenario: Language and Accessibility Preferences

Given diverse Indonesian users
When they access content
Then the system should remember their preferences for font sizes, contrast settings, language options, and reading themes

### Requirement: Content Contribution and Collaboration

The article system SHALL facilitate user-generated content contributions and professional collaboration.

#### Scenario: User-Generated Success Stories

Given patients who have benefited from dental treatments
When they want to share their experiences
Then moderated submission systems should allow them to contribute success stories and testimonials

#### Scenario: Professional Guest Contributions

Given dental professionals wanting to contribute expertise
When they submit content ideas
Then editorial workflows should facilitate collaboration, peer review, and publication of professional guest articles

#### Scenario: Community Content Curation

Given active community members
When they identify valuable external resources
Then community curation systems should allow users to suggest and vote on external articles and resources for inclusion

### Requirement: Analytics and Insights Dashboard

The article system SHALL provide content creators and administrators with comprehensive analytics about content performance and user engagement.

#### Scenario: Content Performance Metrics

Given content creators and editors
When they analyze article performance
Then detailed analytics should show reading time, completion rates, engagement patterns, and social sharing metrics

#### Scenario: User Behavior Insights

Given system administrators
When they review platform usage
Then anonymized user behavior data should reveal popular topics, reading patterns, and areas for content improvement

#### Scenario: Professional Development Analytics

Given educational institutions and professional organizations
When they track student engagement
Then comprehensive reports should show learning progress, popular educational content, and knowledge acquisition metrics
