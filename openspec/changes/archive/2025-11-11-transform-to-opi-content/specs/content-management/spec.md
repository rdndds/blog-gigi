## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Pediatric Dental Health Content Focus

The blog SHALL provide specialized content focusing on children's dental health challenges in the digital age, anti-sugar campaigns, and 3D educational innovations.

#### Scenario: Digital Age Dental Crisis Content

- **WHEN** users visit the homepage or read main articles
- **THEN** content SHALL address "Lonjakan Karies Digital Age" with specific statistics from Riskesdas 2023-2024
- **AND** SHALL include data about screen time impacts, sugar consumption patterns, and brushing frequency
- **AND** SHALL feature Erdinta Ovielia Putri's community screening activities
- **AND** SHALL showcase 3D and audiovisual educational media innovations

#### Scenario: Anti-Sugar Campaign Content

- **WHEN** users read educational content about sugar impacts
- **THEN** content SHALL detail the "Zero Sugar Drink at School" campaign
- **AND** SHALL include statistics about sugar consumption and cavity increases
- **AND** SHALL feature Erdinta's involvement in anti-sugar education activities
- **AND** SHALL demonstrate 3D phantom and print materials for education

#### Scenario: 3D Educational Innovation Content

- **WHEN** users explore educational innovation topics
- **THEN** content SHALL showcase HealthTech Education approaches
- **AND** SHALL detail AR dental models, interactive videos, and sensor-based brushing simulations
- **AND** SHALL feature Erdinta's 3D "Gigi Berlubang Realistis untuk Anak" model
- **AND** SHALL explain video content for dental prosthetics education
